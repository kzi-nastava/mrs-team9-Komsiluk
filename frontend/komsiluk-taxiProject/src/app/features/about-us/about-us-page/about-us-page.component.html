About us

A modern neighborhood ride-hailing platform connecting passengers and drivers in a safe and reliable way.

Neighbourhood Taxi is a ride-hailing service that helps passengers book rides quickly and allows drivers to manage their daily work in a clear and transparent way. The system supports instant bookings, scheduled rides, detailed ride history, and usage reports for all users.
